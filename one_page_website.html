Welcome to Space Mountain

Space Mountain is an indoor roller coaster in Tomorrowland at the Magic Kingdom at Walt Disney World Resort. It opened on January 15, 1975 and was the first Space Mountain attraction at any Disney park. It is unique in that it features two tracks, much like its inspiration, Disneyland's Matterhorn Bobsleds, which opened in 1959.
